Selector: <fd-panel-header>

Applies the panel header style to a div element and serves as a container for the Panel Expand component, the Panel Title and an optional Toolbar component.

<div fd-panel-header>
  <div fd-panel-expand></div>
  <h5 fd-panel-title>Panel Header</h5>
  <div>Other content</div>
</div>
